## What Is Covered

### Regulations and Standards

- **EU:** GDPR, NIS2, DORA, EU SCCs, EDPB guidance, EU CRA (Regulation (EU) 2024/2847)
- **US:** HIPAA, SOC 2, US FAR, CISA
- **International:** PCI DSS, NIST 800-161, ISO 27036, ISO 27701, UNCITRAL, ICC
- **UK:** UK IDTA
- **Threat taxonomy:** CWE/MITRE

### Clause Library

- 28 clause templates with model language across 5 agreement types:
  - NDA (mutual)
  - Service agreements
  - Maintenance and support agreements
  - Software license (proprietary)
  - CRA-specific clauses
- 50 agreement structure sections across: `nda-mutual`, `nda-one-way`, `maintenance-support`, `software-license-proprietary`, `sla`
- 8 CRA contract obligations mapped from Articles 13, 14, 19, 20, and Annex I

### Domain Coverage

- Contract clause taxonomy (369 types across data protection, liability, IP, termination, etc.)
- 147 contract type definitions with required/recommended clause mappings
- Compliance requirement-to-clause mapping for all listed regulations
- Risk patterns with severity, trigger conditions, and remediation
- Negotiation flags (red/amber/green) from buyer and seller perspectives
- Clause interaction analysis (dependencies, conflicts, alignment)
- IP provision library (assignment, licensing, background IP, moral rights, work-for-hire)
- Threat patterns for contract-specific attack scenarios (supply chain, data exfiltration, IP theft)
- Standard framework cross-references (ISO, NIST, EU, PCI SSC)

## What Is NOT Covered

- Full FIDIC construction contracts
- Shipping and charterparty terms
- Insurance contract clauses
- Arbitration rules text (ICC/LCIA/SIAC)
- Employment contract templates
- Real estate lease templates
- M&A transaction documents
- Sector-specific regulatory clauses beyond cybersecurity (e.g., environmental, pharmaceutical, telecommunications)
